# Destructive operations

For anything hard to reverse — dropping a table, `rm -rf`, `mkfs`, force-push, deleting drafts/media, formatting a disk/card — **explain what you're about to do, then STOP and wait for Marty's explicit confirmation** before running it. This is a hard rule, not a suggestion: a Zoom recorder's SD card was formatted prematurely once from skipping this step, and it's not happening again.

This applies on top of (not instead of) the general "confirm before risky actions" behavior — it's called out separately here because it has actually bitten this project before.
